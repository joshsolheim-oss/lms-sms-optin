What this covers

This policy covers SMS text message notifications sent by the Life Management System, a personal productivity and fitness tracking application operated by Josh Solheim. Messages include activity reminders, fitness nudges, and daily health-tracking notifications.

Information collected

The only information collected for this SMS program is the mobile phone number provided at opt-in, used solely to deliver the notifications described above.

No sharing of mobile information

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on activity and time of day, up to approximately 12 messages per day.

Message and data rates

Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-out and help

Reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages. Reply HELP for assistance. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
